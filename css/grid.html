Elephant

The majestic elephant is the largest land animal.
Species: There are three main species of elephants:
African Bush Elephant (Loxodonta africana)
African Forest Elephant (Loxodonta cyclotis)
Asian Elephant (Elephas maximus)


Lion

The lion is known as the king of the jungle.
Lions are majestic and powerful big cats known for their distinctive manes and impressive hunting abilities.
They belong to the genus Panthera and are one of the four big cats in the Panthera genus, alongside tigers, leopards, and jaguars.

Tiger

The tiger is a powerful and majestic big cat species.
Tigers are magnificent and awe-inspiring big cats known for their striking appearance, strength, and agility.
They belong to the genus Panthera and are the largest among the big cat species.
