Apple

Apples are juicy and sweet fruits that come in various colors, such as red, green, and yellow.

They are a rich source of dietary fiber, vitamin C, and various antioxidants.

Banana

Bananas are elongated, curved fruits with a yellow skin when ripe.

They are an excellent source of potassium, vitamin B6, and dietary fiber.

Orange

Oranges are round citrus fruits with a bright orange color.

They are rich in vitamin C, a powerful antioxidant that boosts the immune system.
